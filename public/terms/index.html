2. The Two-Sided Subscription and Monetization Mandate

Velgo’s business model is sustained entirely by Access Subscription Fees paid by both Clients and Workers, in accordance with the selected tier (Basic, Lite, Standard, Pro, Enterprise).

  • Non-Refundable Fee: All subscription fees are paid monthly in advance and are strictly non-refundable, regardless of usage (number of jobs posted or accepted).
  • Enforcement: Access is gated by the subscription tier. Any attempt by a Basic, Lite, Standard, or Pro User to exceed their Job Limit (posted or accepted) will result in an application-level block requiring immediate upgrade.

This section defines the financial relationship and Velgo’s complete disassociation from it:

  • Worker Risk: The Worker acknowledges that they bear the full risk of non-payment or delayed payment by the Client and agrees that their recourse is against the Client, not Velgo.
