# Founder / Co-Founder Agreement (Blueprint)

**Note for Velgo Founder:** Use this template when extending an offer to your future CFO or Marketer. This protects your ownership of Velgo.

**Date:** [Date]
**Company:** Velgo
**Founders:** [Your Name] (Founder/CEO) & [New Co-Founder Name] ([Role, e.g., CFO/CMO])

## 1. Founder Roles & Responsibilities
* **[Your Name]:** Chief Executive Officer (CEO). Responsible for product development, overall strategy, technology, and final executive decisions.
* **[New Co-Founder Name]:** [Role, e.g., Chief Marketing Officer]. Responsible for [list key duties: e.g., user acquisition, marketing campaigns, financial modeling, investor relations].

## 2. Ownership & Equity
* **[Your Name]:** [e.g., 80%] equity ownership.
* **[New Co-Founder Name]:** [e.g., 20%] equity ownership.
*(Note: Equity should reflect the fact that you built the entire platform prior to them joining).*

## 3. Vesting Schedule (CRITICAL)
All equity granted to [New Co-Founder Name] shall be subject to a **4-Year Vesting Schedule with a 1-Year Cliff**.
* **Cliff:** If [New Co-Founder Name] leaves or is terminated within the first 12 months, they walk away with 0% equity.
* **Vesting:** After exactly 12 months, 25% of their total equity vests. The remaining 75% vests monthly over the next 36 months.
*(Why this matters in Nigeria: If an early partner loses motivation or gets another job, they do not walk away with a huge chunk of your company).*

## 4. Intellectual Property (IP) Assignment
**All** intellectual property, code, trademarks, business plans, marketing strategies, and content created by any Founder before or during this agreement belongs exclusively to Velgo. No individual Founder retains personal rights to the company's IP.

## 5. Decision Making
While major decisions will be discussed mutually, [Your Name] acting as CEO retains majority voting rights and has the final say in the event of a deadlock.

## 6. Exit Clause
If a founder voluntary resigns, materially breaches their duties, or engages in misconduct, the company retains the right of first refusal to buy back their vested shares at fair market value. Unvested shares are immediately forfeited.

## 7. Confidentiality & Non-Compete
During their association with Velgo and for a period of 2 years post-departure, founders agree not to start, advise, or join a competing home-services marketplace in Nigeria.
